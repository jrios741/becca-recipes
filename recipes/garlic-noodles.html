Garlic Noodles

One of Becca's favorite foods to get is thai food, specifically that of Richmond's My Bar and Noodle restaurant. Usually, she gets some kind of curry or duck related menu item. But at home, Becca makes these noodles, which in my opinion, are than anything I can get there.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 8 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 oz spurghetti
  • 1/4 cup finely grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
